Additional Rules

Rallying

Cards with the ability “Rallying” begin the scenario in the Rallying state. Rallying formations cannot attack or be attacked. Only one rallying formation can be given dice in a turn, but when rallying a formation’s wing is disregarded for the purposes of assigning dice. Using the Rallying ability does not count as an action, and adds 1 to the unit’s strength. A formation stops Rallying when the card targeted by the Rallying ability is Retired or Routed, and can no longer use the “Rallying” ability. When formations stop Rallying, they retain their dice.

Also, the English (red) go first.
